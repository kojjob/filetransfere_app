Everything you need to know about ZipShare

What is ZipShare?

ZipShare is a next-generation file transfer platform built with Elixir and Phoenix for maximum performance. It offers live progress tracking, resumable uploads, end-to-end encryption, and seamless team collaboration - all designed for creative professionals who need speed and reliability.

What's the maximum file size I can transfer?

ZipShare supports files up to 10GB+ with our intelligent chunked upload system. Large files are automatically split into smaller pieces, uploaded in parallel, and reassembled on the server - ensuring fast, reliable transfers even for massive files.

How does the resumable upload feature work?

Our chunked upload system tracks exactly which pieces of your file have been uploaded. If your connection drops or you close your browser, you can resume right where you left off - no need to start over. This is especially valuable for large files over slow or unreliable connections.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. ZipShare uses 256-bit AES encryption for all files at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. You can also add password protection and set automatic expiration dates for shared links. We never access your files, and you maintain full control over who can download them.

What makes the live progress tracking special?

ZipShare provides live progress updates so you'll see your exact transfer speed, bytes transferred, and accurate time remaining. Our chunked upload system also means you can resume interrupted transfers exactly where you left off.

Can I integrate ZipShare with my existing tools?

Yes! ZipShare offers comprehensive REST API for custom integrations. We also support popular automation platforms like Zapier, Make.com, and n8n. Whether you need to automate client deliverables or build custom workflows, our APIs make it possible.

When will ZipShare be available?

We're currently in private beta and will be launching publicly soon. Join our waitlist to be among the first to get access. Early supporters will receive exclusive benefits including extended trial periods and priority support.

What platforms does ZipShare support?

ZipShare is a web-based application that works on any modern browser - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Native desktop and mobile apps are on our roadmap. Our responsive design ensures a great experience whether you're on desktop, tablet, or mobile.
